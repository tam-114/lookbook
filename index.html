About Me

Want to get to know me
a bit better?

I'm a passionate web developer and designer with a focus on creating diverse, inclusive, and accessible digital experiences. I thrive on the variety of projects that come from working with different industries, and I love the challenge of making sure each project not only meets technical standards but also reflects these core values.

With a strong technical background in front-end development, I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery, crafting responsive, user-friendly websites. I’m skilled in PHP, MySQL, and WordPress, with experience in domain name configuration and working with various content management systems (CMS). My design expertise includes Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe XD, allowing me to merge functionality and aesthetics seamlessly.
