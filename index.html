About Me

Looking to get to know me
a bit better?

I'm a passionate Web Designer & Developer who's all about creating websites to be digital solutions and experiences. I love working with different industries, taking on new challenges, and making sure every project not only meets technical standards but also has a unique touch.

My front-end development skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery, allowing me to build responsive, user-friendly websites. I also work with PHP, MySQL, and WordPress, handling everything from custom development to domain setup and CMS management. On the design side, I use Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva to bring a perfect balance of function and aesthetics to my work.

Mental Health

Black Therapist & Company

I worked with Black Therapist and Company, a mental health clinic, to create a website that makes it easy for both patients and providers to access important information, find resources, and stay connected.

What I Accomplished:

  • Domain and Hosting Setup: Selected a custom domain and set up reliable hosting to give the clinic a strong and professional online presence.
  • Custom WordPress Website: Designed and developed a clean, easy-to-navigate website that aligns with their mission and helps users find what they need quickly.
  • Logo Design: Created a unique logo that represents the clinic's identity and utilizes their painting.
  • Email Configuration: Configured professional email accounts to be HIPPA compliant and secure communication between the clinic and its clients.
Result: The clinic now has a professional online platform that supports their work while keeping privacy and user experience a top priority.
