stemCONNECT Internship

Overview

The goal of stemCONNECT is to inspire students to pursure STEM careers, and my role in Workforce Development is to assist in the outreach initiatives of bringing experts in STEM careers into the classroom virtually. I am an organizer and host of stemCONNECT sessions and assist in facilitating events such as the Virtual College Tour and Themed Months events. I create social media graphics and newsletters for promotional material, and I update the stemCONNECT webpage for our latest content. This internship has taught me many things, notably improving my communication skills in all forms and strengthening my creativity in graphic and web design.



My Experience as a UCF Student

CODE Orlando reached out to us for a speaker that could present for their middle school students about what it's like to be a UCF student. I volunteered to talk about my academic journey, major, and the programs and events that UCF offered. It was a great experience to be able to interact with them and answer their questions, I hoped to inspire them to pursure STEAM careers in the future.



Themed Month: Hurricane/Meteorolgy Month Graphic

For the 2022-2023 school year, we are implementing the Themed Month program, where speakers in relation to the topics chosen for the month can volunteer to present their careers and give an in-depth look into their careers. Classrooms will be invited to attend these sessions, where students have the opportunity to ask questions and learn more about lesson topics. This is one of the graphics for social media that advertises the themed month of September.

Themed Months Newsletter

For the Themed Months program, we are creating a newsletter for Experts to receive updates on ways to stay connected by following our social media, sign up to be a volunteer speaker for the program, and encouraged to spread the word of stemCONNECT and our goals to their network to expand our impact.



Key Advice: Re-Editing Videos

My task was to create compilations centered around a topic, such as a video with the topic of key advice to students. I used footage from 2 interviews from IT experts and compiled them into a 2 minute video. This was important because we are creating content for our social media pages for more outreach and re-purposing videos that used to be longer in length, but shortened for Tik Tok, Instagram, etc.
